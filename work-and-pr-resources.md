# 🌍 Official Work & Immigration Websites

Here is a curated list of official government portals for work permits, visas, and immigration across different regions.

---

## 🇪🇺 Europe (Schengen / European Union)

*   🇮🇪 **Ireland:** [Department of Enterprise, Trade and Employment](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/?)
    - Connecting Employers and Jobseekers [JobsIreland](https://jobsireland.ie/)
*   🇩🇪 **Germany:** [Make it in Germany - Official Portal for Qualified Professionals](https://www.make-it-in-germany.com/en/)
*   🇳🇱 **Netherlands:** [IND - Immigration and Naturalisation Service](https://ind.nl/en)
*   🇸🇪 **Sweden:** [Swedish Migration Agency (Migrationsverket)](https://www.migrationsverket.se/English/Private-individuals/Working-in-Sweden.html)
*   🇪🇺 **EU Blue Card:** [Official EU Blue Card Network](https://www.ec.europa.eu/immigration/blue-card/essential-information_en)

---

## 🇨🇦 North America

*   🇨🇦 **Canada:** [IRCC - Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship.html)
*   🇨🇦 **Canada Express Entry:** [Check Eligibility & Express Entry Portal](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry.html)

---

## 🇦🇺 Oceania

*   🇦🇺 **Australia:** [Department of Home Affairs - Visa and Immigration](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/)
*   🇦🇺 **Australia SkillSelect:** [SkillSelect Points-Based Immigration Portal](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/working-in-australia/skillselect)
*   🇳🇿 **New Zealand:** [Immigration New Zealand](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/)

---

## 🌏 Asia

*   🇰🇷 **South Korea:** [Korea Visa Portal (Official Government Site)](https://www.visa.go.kr/)
*   🇯🇵 **Japan:** [Immigration Services Agency of Japan](https://www.isa.go.jp/en/index.html)
*   🇸🇬 **Singapore:** [Ministry of Manpower (MOM) - Work Passes and Visas](https://www.mom.gov.sg/)

---
> 💡 **Tip:** Always rely strictly on these official `.gov` or official institutional domains to avoid misinformation and fraud during your application process.
